Dog Breeds
Labrador Retriever

Friendly medium-sized dog that is good with kids and enjoys being active and going outside.
German Sheperd

Strong and protective dog with great eyesight.
Golden Retriever

Easily trained and protective breed with a nice coat of low-maintenance fur.
French Bulldog

Small and affectionate dog that is good for apartments.
Bulldog

Classy breed that enjoys being lazy.
Beagle

Cute and energetic breed with many colors.
Poodle

Small and affectionate dog that is good for apartments.
Rottweiler

Big and adaptable breed that is good for kids and best suited for more open spaces.
Yorkshire Terrier

Small, loving and confident dog with a long lifespan and a nice coat of fur.
German Shorthaired Pointer

Dog that is good for families or hunting that enjoys the great outdoors and has an easy to brush fur coat.
